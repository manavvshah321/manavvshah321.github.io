Chess

I started playing chess at 12 years old by joining a chess coaching class. I really enjoyed it and started playing regularly ever since. After starting college, I have stopped playing until the recent show Queens Gambit came out. After that I have started playing and learning again. My favorite chess player is Magnus Carlsen - he is a Norwegian Chess Grandmaster. I have watched many of his games and it was interesting to see how his thought process worked.
